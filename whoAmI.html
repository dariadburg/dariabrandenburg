Experience. The experience of the viewer.
Can you change the perception? How? Experience and perception.

I am sharing with you my experience. But, do you perceive it as I do? No. You create your own perspective of it. That is what I want to do. Leave you wonder on the what ifs. Make them your own. Without you, my work is unfinished. You are the one who completes it.

I am in constant exploration. There is so much to question and understand but I begin to understand myself. I gain insights in my way of working and interests. They can be broad. However, it all comes down to one element: experience.
